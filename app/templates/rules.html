Rules of Conduct

When using Boxed In, please keep these rules in mind:

Content uploaded must be appropriate and inoffensive, i.e. no cyberbullying, doxxing, etc.
Do not use photos of other students or staff without their consent.
Do not share any personal information on this platform. Remember that any content sent in boxes will be shared on public feeds when opened!
Do not share any recognisable information about other members of the school community outside of Boxed In.

Boxed In is a supportive platform and we take student security seriously. If you break any of these rules of conduct, your account and any content sent by you will be removed.

By using Boxed In, you agree to abide by these rules.
